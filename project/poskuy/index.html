A Comprehensive Point of Sale Application

An advanced Point of Sale (POS) application designed to streamline and enhance your business operations. With seamless integration of technologies such as RoomDatabase, Firebase Realtime Database, and Firebase Analytics, PoSkuy is your ultimate solution for efficient inventory management, multi-unit and multi-price stock handling, and unit conversion calculations.

Features

Inventori Management

PoSkuy empowers you to effortlessly manage your inventory. Keep track of all your products, their details, and availability at your fingertips. With real-time updates from Firebase Realtime Database, you'll always have accurate inventory information.

Multi-Unit & Multi-Price Support

Say goodbye to limitations. PoSkuy is equipped to handle products with multiple units and prices. Easily configure and manage different units and pricing tiers for a single product, catering to diverse customer needs.

Unit Conversion

PoSkuy simplifies the complex. The unit conversion feature ensures smooth transitions between units by adhering to predefined ratios. This ensures precision in your transactions, allowing you to serve customers seamlessly regardless of their preferred unit.

Firebase Integration

Leveraging the power of Firebase, PoSkuy offers real-time synchronization of data across devices. This means that any changes made on one device will be instantly reflected across all other devices, promoting efficient decision-making and reducing errors.

Analytics Insights

Firebase Analytics is seamlessly integrated into PoSkuy, providing you with valuable insights into your business operations. Understand customer behavior, track sales trends, and optimize your strategies based on real-time data.

User-Friendly Interface

PoSkuy boasts an intuitive and user-friendly interface, making it accessible to both novice users and experienced retailers. With easy navigation and a clean design, you can focus on managing your business rather than dealing with complicated software.

Whether you're a small business owner, a retail professional, or an entrepreneur looking to streamline your sales processes, PoSkuy is the comprehensive solution that empowers you to efficiently manage your inventory, offer flexible pricing, and provide a seamless shopping experience for your customers.

Elevate your business with PoSkuy today and experience the difference firsthand. Simplify inventory management, optimize pricing strategies, and embrace the future of retail with confidence.

Enhanced Key Features of PoSkuy: Empowering Your Business

In addition to its core features, PoSkuy goes above and beyond to provide an all-inclusive solution for your business management needs. Here are the enhanced key features that make PoSkuy a game-changer

Category Management

Seamlessly organize your products by categories to enhance user experience and simplify navigation. Efficiently manage and sort your inventory for quick access and streamlined operations.

Debt and Receivable Management

PoSkuy allows manual input and automated integration of debt and receivable data. Keep track of owed amounts, payments, and outstanding balances to ensure transparency in financial dealings.

Cash Flow Management

Take control of your financial inflow and outflow outside the POS application. Log manual entries for income and expenses to maintain an accurate overview of your financial health.

Daily and Weekly Reports

PoSkuy provides comprehensive daily and weekly reports to give you a holistic view of your business performance. These reports include:

  • Total income from sales, debts, and cash inflow.
  • Total expenses from stock purchases, receivables, and cash outflow.
  • Sales performance graph showcasing your revenue trends over time.
  • Best-selling products based on quantities sold, helping you make informed restocking decisions.
